Clickstarter is a platform for funding causes without asking for money

We give up millions of hours a year to social media, ads, and apps with nothing to show for it. Why not use that time for good?

Together, we can channel our attention in a way that drives the causes we care about: charities, entrepreneurs, sports teams, clubs, the list goes on...

There's no obligation and no commitment - just hop on and try out a survey, app, or ad - that's all it takes to lend some much-needed support to a great cause!
